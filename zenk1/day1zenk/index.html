Making Pancakes

  1. Flour: This homemade pancake recipe starts with all-purpose flour.
  2. Baking powder: Baking powder, a leavener, is the secret to fluffy pancakes
  3. Sugar: Just a tablespoon of white sugar is all you'll need for subtly sweet pancakes
  4. Salt: A pinch of salt will enhance the overall flavor without making your pancakes taste salty.
  5. Milk and butter: Milk and butter add moisture and richness to the pancakes.
  6. Egg: A whole egg lends even more moisture. Plus, it helps bind the pancake batter together.
